Shipping Policy

Shipping expectations for London Fit™ orders.

This page explains processing times, delivery windows and what customers should expect after placing an order.

Order processing

Orders are usually processed within 1 to 3 business days after payment confirmation. Processing times may be longer during high-volume periods, launches or promotional events.

Delivery estimates

Estimated delivery windows depend on destination, carrier conditions and customs where applicable. Delivery estimates shown at checkout are not guaranteed delivery dates.

Shipping coverage

Availability of shipping options may vary by country or region. If you are focusing on London and UK customers, update this policy to reflect the exact areas you serve.

Tracking

Where tracking is available, customers will receive shipping confirmation and tracking details after dispatch.

Delays

Carrier delays, customs inspections, weather conditions and peak seasons may affect transit times. London Fit™ is not responsible for delays caused by third-party carriers once an order has been dispatched.

Incorrect shipping details

Please make sure your address is complete and accurate before placing an order. We cannot guarantee changes after an order has been submitted.

Lost or missing parcels

If your parcel appears lost or tracking stops updating for an unusual length of time, contact support so we can review the shipment and next steps.
