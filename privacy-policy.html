Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters at London Fit™.

This page explains what information we collect, how we use it and how we protect it when you browse, buy or contact us.

What we collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, billing details, order history and any information you choose to share in reviews, contact forms or support messages.

How we use your data

We use your information to process orders, arrange delivery, send order updates, respond to support requests, prevent fraud and improve the shopping experience on our website.

Payments and checkout

Payments are processed through secure third-party payment providers. We do not store full card details on our website. Payment data is handled through secure checkout infrastructure.

Marketing communication

If you subscribe to updates, we may send emails about launches, offers or brand news. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in the email or by contacting us directly.

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies, pixels and analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site, improve performance, measure campaigns and personalise the experience.

Data protection

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, misuse, disclosure or loss.

Your rights

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct inaccurate information or request deletion where legally permitted.

Contact

If you have any privacy-related questions, update this page with your business support email so customers know how to reach you directly.
