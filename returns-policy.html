Returns Policy

Simple returns guidance for London Fit™ orders.

This page explains when returns may be accepted, what condition items should be in and how refunds are handled.

Return window

Customers have 15 days from delivery to request a return. Return requests made after this window may not be accepted.

Return condition

Returned items should be unworn, unwashed and in their original condition, with tags and packaging where possible. Items showing signs of wear may not qualify for a refund.

How to request a return

Customers should contact support with their order number, delivery details and reason for return before sending anything back.

Refunds

Once the return is received and inspected, approved refunds are usually sent back to the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on the payment provider.

Exchanges

Exchanges may be available for another size or colour where stock allows. If the requested option is unavailable, we may offer a refund instead.

Non-returnable cases

Items may not be eligible if they are damaged after use, returned too late or do not meet hygiene or wear-condition requirements.

Return shipping

Return shipping is normally paid by the customer unless the item arrives faulty or incorrect. Please contact support before sending any item back.
